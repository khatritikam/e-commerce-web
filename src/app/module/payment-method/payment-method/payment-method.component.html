PAYMENT METHODS

We offer various payment methods in the Org Farm online shop:

Credit And Debit Cards:

We accept payments from all major credit and debit card networks like Visa, Mastercard and RuPay.

Netbanking:

We accept payments through 50+ banks including SBI, ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, Axis Bank, Kotak Bank and Yes Bank. The bank page opens in a pop-up window so you can easily come back to our website, assured of a great shopping experience.

Payment Wallets & UPI:

We allow our customers to pay via popular online wallets like Mobikwik, Freecharge and Ola Money. You can approve payments using a one-time password (OTP) directly on checkout page for a seamless experience.

Cash On Delivery:

Cash on Delivery can be used as a payment method for orders shipping all over India. There will be an additional fee of Rs. 150 that will be charged for every cash on delivery order.
