shipping & return policy

  1. Shipping

    We strive to deliver items purchased from Org Farm.com in excellent condition and within the shortest time possible. Shipping is entirely free for prepaid purchase of items through the website. A charge of Rs.150 is applicable for cash-on-delivery (COD) orders.

  2. MODIFYING SHIPPING ADDRESS AFTER PLACING ORDER

    The shipping address for an order can be modified before the order gets processed and dispatched. The customer may update it under the Change Address section which is available under the My Order section of the website.

  3. CANCELLED ORDERS AND REFUNDS

    Once an order is shipped, the cancel option will not be available on the website. In case of prepaid orders for which payment has been made via credit/debit card, net banking or My Wallet on the website, the customer will receive complete refund in the source account within 7 to 10 days of order cancellation.

  4. RETURNS & EXCHANGES POLICY

    A customer may return/exchange unworn shoes on Org Farm.com for any valid reason, within 15 hours from the date of delivery of the product. Any return/exchange request after the specified period will not be valid. Any unworn shoes can be exchanged due to incorrect size or receipt of a defective item, and the customer will be provided with a replacement of the item, free of any shipping charges. Once an exchange request is initiated, our delivery representative will pick up the original item and will deliver the new item at a later date.

    These steps will be part of the returning/exchanging of items:

    • Pick-up: In most locations, we offer a free pick-up service. You will see a pick-up option when you submit a return request.
    • Self-shipping: A customer may also opt to do self-shipping of items to be returned/exchanged, subject to the Returns and Exchanges Policy.
    • Refund: Once the item has been received at our warehouse and has passed a mandatory quality check, the refund will be initiated. The refund will be done through the customer's bank account in which case he/she should select the account to which refund should be made. In case of COD orders being returned, the same should be done by providing details of one's bank account.

  5. UNDELIVERABLE ORDERS

    An order may be undeliverable if:

    • the package is damaged in transit.
    • delivery is refused by the recipient.
    • the address given is incorrect.
    • there is no recipient to accept the order.

    Our courier partners will not be responsible for retrieval and delivery of undelivered orders. All undeliverable orders will be returned to the source and a full refund will be processed if the payment has already been made online. If a customer wishes to re-purchase the item, he/she may re-order it through the website.
