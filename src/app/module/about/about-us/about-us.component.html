Organic farmers and food producers grow and produce food without using synthetic chemicals such as pesticides and artificial fertilisers. They do not use genetically modified (GM) components or expose food to irradiation. Animal welfare and environmental sustainability are important issues for organic farmers.

A leader is someone who is able to bring people together and help them work together to achieve a common goal. Leaders have the ability to motivate their team members and get them to work together towards a common goal. They also have the ability to make decisions that will affect the entire team. A good leader is able to balance their own needs with those of their team members. In business, it is important to have a clear leader who sets the direction for the company and ensures that everyone follows. This leader should be able to communicate effectively and set goals that are achievable for everyone in the company. They should also be able to motivate their team members and get them up to speed on what is needed to meet the goal.
